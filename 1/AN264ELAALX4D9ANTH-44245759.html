รายละเอียดของสินค้า 10 Gigabit Ultra Flat Cat-7 Ethernet Cables for Modem Router LAN Network​ 1.8M - intl

This Grand 10 Gigabit Ultra Flat Cat-7 Ethernet Cables are expertly designed to meet and surpass today's Ethernet cable standards.

Features:
1. Transmitting data at speeds of up to 10Gbps over 100m run of cable
2. Made of 4 shielded twisted pair (STP) of copper wires with two RJ45 connectors on each end. Compared to the Cat 6 network Ethernet cable, the additional shielding and improved quality in twisting of the wires provides better protection from crosstalk, noise, and interference that can degrade the signal quality
3. The clip protector keeps the RJ45 connector from unwanted snags while routing the cable
4. Surf the net, stream video, music, and other data at greater speed without worrying about cable mess
5. Slim and Flexible
With a thickness of only 0.07 inch, these cables fit easily between cramped spaces. They will easily camouflage under carpets, up walls, and behind furniture, improving the look of your home or office. Its slim profile is also ideal for network rack wiring, giving plenty of ventilation room. The slim design is a great advantage over fat cables, which only add extra bulk to your work space.
6. Fast and Reliable
The Grand Ultra Flat Cat-7 Ethernet Cables are capable of transmitting data at speeds of up to 10 gigabits per second over 100 meters of cable. They are built with shielded twisted pair STP cables and shielded RJ45 connectors. Each twisted pair is shielded with Aluminum Mylar Foil to protect against EMI/RFI interference - Plus a drain wire for grounding. 
7. Designed for all Networking Applications
Designed for Network Adapters, Hubs, Switches, Routers, DSL/Cable Modems, Patch Panels and other Computer Networking applications. Utilize your internet at superior speeds, without the clutter of bulky wiring.

1 x Cat-7 Ethernet Cables

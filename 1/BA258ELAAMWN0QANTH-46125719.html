รายละเอียดของสินค้า USB Extension Ethernet RJ45 Cat5e/6 Cable LAN Adapter Extender Over Repeater Set

Connect USB peripherals to your computer from up to 150 feet (45 meters) away with this adapter. Use an Ethernet patch cable/straight-thru cable (Cat-5, Cat-5e, or Cat-6) to connect the two adapters together.

Note: The distance and presence of adapters may affect the performance of some devices. With distance more than approximately 5 feet (1.5 meters), you may need to use a powered USB hub, as the USB port on your computer does not supply enough power to stretch over distance. Powered USB hub not included as part of this package.

This adapter set is to be used with a patch cable, also called a straight-through cable. The adapters cannot be used separately.

Features:

Overcome the length limits of USB cables with this Plug & Play adapter Connect USB devices up to 150 feet away to your computer by going over Cat-5/5e/6 patch cable **Cat-5/5e/6 patch cable not included!** Use with webcams, mice, keyboards, any device that has a USB plug USB 1.1 transfer speed

Package includes:

1x 1 set of USB extension adapters over RJ45"

12+16pin 2014 2015 Macbook to M.2 NGFF M-Key SSD Convert Card for A1493 A1502 A1465 A1466

The adapter can covert the NGFF M-key SSD to the12+16pin 2014 2015 Macbook SSD.

Note: Only support M-key AHCI SSD, don't support NVME SSD.


Compatibility With:

2013 Macbook Pro A1493 A1502 ( MD864 MD865 MD293 Md294) SSD

2013 MacBook Pro retina A1398 A1502 (ME864 ME865 ME866 ME293 ME294) SSD

2013 Macbook Air A1465 A1466 ( MD711 MD712 MD760 MD761) SSD

2014 Macbook Air A1465 A1466 ( MD711 MD712 MD760 MD761) SSD

2014 MacBook Pro retina A1502 A1398 (MGX72 MGX82 MGX92 MGXA2 MGXC2) SSD

2015 MacBook Pro retina A1502 A1398(MF839 MF840 MF841 MGXA2 MGXC2) SSD

2015 MacBook Air A1465 A1466(MJVM2 MJVP2 MJVE2 MJVG2)
and Mac Pro ME253 MD878 SSD



Note:The adapter only work forM-keySSD, cannot work forB-key or B/M-keysocket.

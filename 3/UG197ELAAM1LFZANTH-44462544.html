Description 

UGREEN DVI 24+5 DVI-I Dual Link to VGA Male to Male cable offers you a cost-effective way to connect an analog or dual mode Flat Panel Display to a PC or Mac computer with a DVI Analog Video Card at resolution up to 1080P. The slim DVI & VGA connectors with screws not only support secure connection, but also easy plugging & unplugging.

3. Supports analog signal only, and DVI & VGA do not support audio output; 
4. With Triple shielding and gold-plated connectors to optimize its performance and minimize cross talk, ensuring high-speed, error-free transmission;

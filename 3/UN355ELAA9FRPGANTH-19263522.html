HDMI to Output VGA Cable Converter

 

The latest version of the VGA to HDMI converter allowing you to connect PS4, XBOX One, XBOX 360, Sky HD, Apple TV, TiVo and other HDMI devices to a VGA monitor, projector or other display. Output definition of 720P and 1080P are supported.

 

1080P HDMI to VGA SVGA RGB Video (Doesn’t Support Audio). The built in chipset separates the HDMI signal and converts the Video for use on the VGA output port. Perfect for use with older monitors and televisions.

 Features:

 ● Latest version

 

● Integrated chipset, no software required, plug and play technology ● Precision fit connectors protect ports and ensure a reliable connection ● Convert HDMI to VGA ● Durable ABS Construction ● HDMI version 1.3 compliant and HDCP compliant ● Compatible with all versions of HDMI up to 720p and 1080p ● Input resolution accepted: 480i/480p/576i/576p/720p/1080i/1080p ● No external power supply required ● Very light weight ● Max resolution is 1920 x 1200 at 60hz

รายละเอียดของสินค้า UGREEN 3-in-1 DisplayPort DP to HDMI / VGA / DVI Adapter Cable Converter - White - intl

Description
UGREEN 3-in-1 DisplayPort DP to HDMI / VGA / DVI Adapter Cable Converter is an indispensable companion for your laptop or desktop equipped with DisplayPort. Connect your computer to an HDTV for high-definition video (4K via HDMI) and audio streaming or to an HD monitor/projector with this portable and versatile adapter and an HDMI/DVI/VGA cable (not include). Use this adapter to enjoy movies on the big HDTV, mirror or extend your desktop for an expanded workstation or show presentations at school or work. 

Connection Notes 
- Input: DisplayPort Male 
- Output: HDMI Female; DVI Female; VGA Female; An HDMI/DVI/VGA cable (sold separately) is required 
- Can only convert signal from DisplayPort to HDMI/DVI/VGA. This is not a bi-directional adapter 
- Connect up to 2 monitors using AMD Eyefinity Multi-Display technology (one adapter per monitor); An active adapter is required to support more monitors 
- It does not support more than one output simultaneously 
- No audio output from VGA and DVI 
- Depress the button on the DisplayPort connector to release the latches and avoid damage when unplugging 

Important Note 
- This adapter is NOT compatible with USB port on your computer 
- It does not support more than one output simultaneously

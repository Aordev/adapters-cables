รายละเอียดของสินค้า CAT7 Double Shielded (SSTP) 10 Gigabit 600MHz Ethernet LAN Network Flat Cable High Speed Patch Cord - Built with Copper Plated & Shielded RJ45 Connectors (50ft/15m, Black) - intl

This Cat7 Ethernet Cable follows the 10GBBASE-T standard and with a blazing speed of up to 600MHz so you can connect to your LAN/WAN segments and networking gear at max speeds.
The Double Shielded Twist Pair (SSTP) design and gold plated connectors confidently protects the cable from external Electro-Magnetic Interference (EMI) for optimized signal quality. The clip protector keeps the RJ45 connector from unwanted snags while routing the cable.

Please note, your network speed is determined by many factors ,which include the cable, router, switch, other network components, and also service provider.

Specifications:

High Performance
Designed for use with 10 Gigabit Ethernet, provides performances of up to 600MHz and speeds of up to 10Gbps.

Interference Elimination
The CAT7 ethernet cable is terminated with 2 shielded gold plated RJ45 connectors. Multi-protection mechanism significantly improves noise resistance and interference elimination

Gold Plated
Built with 50 Micron Gold Plated RJ45 Connectors & Contact Pins. Gold is a natural conductor. When used on connectors and contacts, it achieves greater performance through better connectivity and less signal loss providing up to 20% more efficiency.

Slim and Flat
The CAT7 cables slide under the carpet without raising it or showing that it is under there to improving your home or office, can make it easy to fit between spaces, which are perfect camouflage underneath carpets, walls, or even behind furniture.

Features:
- Cable Type: CAT7 4-Pair FTP 100% Bare Copper
- Connector: Gold Plated RJ45(8P8C)
- Cable Performance: Up to 600MHz
- Transmission Rate: 10 Gigabit
- Conductor Gauge: 32AWG
- Size: Width 7.4mm x Thickness 2.4mm(approx.)
- Certification: ISO/IEC 11801; TIA/EIA-568-B.2-1; RoHS

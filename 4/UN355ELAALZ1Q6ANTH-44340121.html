รายละเอียดของสินค้า TMISHION USB3.0 PCI-E Express 1x to 16x Adapter Extender Riser Card Graphics Card 4Pin SATA Power Cable - intl Description:
This PCI-E 1x to16x graphics card extension cable with fixed large 4pin interface can solve the problems that the welding point of traditional cable is easy to burn, transmission signal attenuation, cable length not enough and so on. This product adopts molding choke and solid capacitors, and the power conversion efficiency is up to 95%.  

Features:
1. PCI-E connector adopting multi-layer shielded wire, the signal will not attenuate when connecting up to 3 meters.
2. With 3x FP solid capacitors, making graphics card power supply more stable and secure.
3. Graphics card with fixed large 4pin interface, strengthen power supply, and reduce the burden of the motherboard.
4. Gold plated contacts for better connectivity and long life; Wire is relatively soft, easy to track.
5. Slot with a fixed buckle, easy to remove or fix graphics card, and the graphics card will not fall off from the slot.
 
Specifications:
Name: Sixth-generation PCI-E 1x to 16x Extension Cable A Male Up
Interface: PCI-E 1x
Applied Interface: PCI-E 1x 4x 8x 16x
USB Cable Length: Approx. 60cm / 23.62inch
Color: Green
Package Weight: 83g

Package List:
1 x PCI-E 16x Adapter
1 x PCI-E 1x Riser Board
1 x USB 3.0 Cable
1 x SATA 15Pin to 4Pin Power Cable

Note:
1. Please Plug correctly to avoid burning your equipment.
2. Please hold the sock when plug wire to avoid damaging the socket pin because of overexerting.
3. 1x to 16x when using only the 1x signal, some devices may not recognize. Please understand.
4. Light shooting and different displays may cause the color of the item in the picture a little different from the real thing. The measurement allowed error is 1-3 cm.

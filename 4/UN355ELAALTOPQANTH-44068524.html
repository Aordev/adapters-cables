Description:
The item is 2 slots adapter Card: M key M.2 NGFF SSD to PCI-E X4 adapter and B key M.2 NGFF SSD to SATA adapter.

Features:
1. M key M.2 NGFF SSD to PCI-E X4 adapter or B key  M.2 NGFF SSD to SATA adapter.
2. The speed and other properties is not changed, only change the interface type and without any software or drivers. 
3. Support size: 22*30 mm, 22*42 mm, 22*60 mm, 22*80 mm M.2 NGFF SSD.
4. Macth with two kinds of Metal sheet ( length:8 cm, 12 cm )
5. Compatible with any operating system ( On the premise of operating system must be compatible with your hard disk ).

1. Socket 2(B key 2 LANE):there are SATA(such as THNSNH128G8NT/ THNSNH128GDNT/MZNTD256HAGM models of SSD) and PCI-E 2X(such as PX-AG128M6E/PX-AG256M6E/PX-AG512M6E/SD6PP4M models of SSD) two different transfer mode.

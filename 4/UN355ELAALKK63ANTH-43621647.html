HDMI to VGA cable is designed to connect and transfer (Not Converter) signals from HDMI port to VGA port.This cable functions as a signal transmitter, but NOT a signal converter. It is an ideal cable for high-definition DVD players, HDTV receivers, TV sets, projectors, A/V receivers, and other HDMI-equipped devices. Gold plated connectors make sure superior and stable signal transfer; Cable transmits video signals up to Full HD 1080P.
    
Features:
1.Supports video resolutions up to Full HD 1080P. Connectors to ensure superior signal transfer.
2.Transmit signal from HDMI devices (Laptop, PC, PS3/4, Xbox, DVD, TV Box) to VGA devices (Monitors, Projects, TV).
3.HDMI is digital signal and VGA is analog signal, and the cable does not convert the signal. The devices themselves are the ones that need to convert the signal. If the devices do not support coding and decoding then buy an adapter separately.

4.This is a HDMI to VGA one-way transmission cable, VGA to HDMI cannot be used, which means the display device (TV or monitor) is the VGA end.


Specifications:

Color: Black

Connectivity: 1 x VGA 15pin Male, 1 x HDMI Male

Cable Length: 1.5M / 5FT(approx).

NOTE1: Even though HDMI cables support Hot Plug Detection, improper usage might result in resetting / restarting both devices, or even may cause damage to the devices. Therefore, we do not suggest Hot Plug action for any HDMI cables. Please make sure both input and output devices are off when plugging or unplugging HDMI cables.

NOTE2:Not all of devices with HDMI or VGA interface is able to use this cable.
If both input / output device do not support signal conversion, a signal conversion box is required. It doesn't work with laptops to LCD monitor directly and that you will need an adapter box to convert the VGA signals to HDMI in the first place. Only our cable is not enough.It only fits High Definition Player, converter is needed when connect the PC and TV.
